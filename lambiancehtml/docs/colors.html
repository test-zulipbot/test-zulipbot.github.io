A how-to guide to get the most out of your new template.

Colors Schemes

The template comes in two different color schemes: white and black.

Less

Leave things the way they are if you want to use the black scheme. If you want to switch to white do the following: Open style.less and replace @import 'theme/colorscheme/default by @import 'theme/colorscheme/light' Your theme should now switch to the white color scheme.

Css

On each and every page instead of assets/css/style.css simply include assets/css/style-white.css. Your theme should now switch to the white color scheme.

Colors

Ambiance makes use of only one color. You can change it in one of the following two ways:

Less

Open the file theme/colorscheme/default.less (or light.less) and adjust the variable @color-secondary to your desired value.

Css

Open the file assets/style.css and search and replace the value #6C859E to the value you desire.
